About Us

Who we are

Masterscan Engineering Pte Ltd registered in April 2005 by a group of highly competent NDT professionals with knowledge and experience from wide range of industries… Oil and Gas, Petrochemical, Power plants, Refineries, Rail road, Marine, Construction and Heavy Engineering industries.

Accrediation

We are independent testing company accredited by Singapore Accreditation Council-Singapore Laboratory Accreditation Scheme(SAC SINGLAS) to ISO/IEC 17025.We are also approved testing centre by DET NORSKE VERITAS to carry out Non-Destructive Testing of offshore and ships structures.

Masterscan

Our Services

NDT Services

We provide services in the field of NDT to cater the needs of industries ranging from small Engineering, Marine, Oil & Gas Industries.

Training & Certification

We provide a specialized training (RT, UT, MT , PT) equipped with up to date instructional materials & fully established NDT lab for complete practicals.

Marine Inspection

Our Team is professionally equipped to carry our thickness surveys on all kinds of ships such as Oil tankers, Oil Rigs, FPSO Vessels and Drill Ships.

Engineering Consultancy

The combination of practical experience and the theory rich knowledge of certified ASNT NDT professional within our training laboratory atmosphere strike the perfect hands-on balanced training experience.

Onshore & Offshore Inspections

We do Tank Inspection, Tube Inspection, Under water Inspection. We do all type of surveys either Onshore or Offshore.
